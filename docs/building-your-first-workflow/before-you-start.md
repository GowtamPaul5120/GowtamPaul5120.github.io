# Before You Start

When you log in, you automatically join a public workspace, where you can practice creating applications and workflows.

:::info
You can create your first workflow even in an existing application, but we recommend you create your own application, just to ensure you don't modify the work of other users.&#x20;
:::

## Create Your Application

To create an application:

1. Click **Create Application** in the Home page, or go to the **Applications** page from the main menu and click **Create Application**. The **Create Application** modal appears.
2. Click **New Application**. The **Create Application** modal appears.
3. Enter _HelloWorld_ in the **Name** field. Leave the other fields unchanged.
4. Click **Create**.

Your application is now ready, and you can [add workflows](create-your-first-workflow.md) to it.
