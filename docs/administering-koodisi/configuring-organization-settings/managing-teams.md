---
title: "Managing Teams"
---


Use the Settings > Organization > Teams page to manage teams in your organization. You can assign a unique set of permissions to every team in Koodisi, and all member of a team inherit those permissions.&#x20;

For example, you can create a team of administrators and assign that team permissions to perform the most basic administrative tasks, ensuring that any administrator who's part of that team can perform them. You can then assign additional permissions to each administrator in the team, offering them finer-grained access to data and tasks as needed.
