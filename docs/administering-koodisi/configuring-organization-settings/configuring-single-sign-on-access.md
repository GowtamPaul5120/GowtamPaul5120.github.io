---
title: "Configuring Single Sign-On Access"
---


Implement SSO to let your organization's users log into Koodisi directly with their company credentials, simplifying access and enhancing security.

## Enabling SSO

To activate SSO for your organization, simply toggle the **Customize SSO Access** switch to the **On** position.

## Enabling SSO for Specific Users

By default, SSO is disabled for all users even after you enble SSO access for your organization.&#x20;

To enable SSO access for a specific employee, toggle their **Enable SSO Login** switch to the **On** position. Click **Save**.
