# Editing Teams

To update the details associated with a team:

1. Navigate to the Settings > Organization > Teams page.
2. Search for the team you want to edit, and click the **Actions** button adjacent to the team and select **Edit** from the options that appear.

:::info
When you edit a team, you can update all its details, including its name, description, associated permissions, and the list of members.
:::

## Removing Team Members from a Team

To remove a team member from a team:

1. Edit the team to display the Edit Team page.&#x20;
2. Click the **Delete** icon adjacent to the user in the Edit Team page.
3. Click **Delete Member** in the warning message that appears.
