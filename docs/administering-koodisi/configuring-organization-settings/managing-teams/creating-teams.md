---
title: Creating Teams
---


To create a team:

1. Navigate to the Settings > Organization > Teams page and click the **Create** button.\
   The Create Team side panel appears.
2. Enter a **Team Name** and, optionally, add a **Description** for the team.
3. Use the **Organization Permissions** section to assign specific permissions to every member of the team.
4. Click the **Members** tab to add members to the team.\
   The Team Users page appears.
5. Click the **Add Members** button.\
   The Add Team Collaborators side panel appears.
6. Use the **Add Members** field to search for team members. Click on each member to add them to the team.\
   As you add team members, you can see their names appearing in the Team Members table below the Add Members field.
7. Click **Add**.\
   Your team members are now added, and the Create Team page reappears.&#x20;
8. Click **Save Team**.&#x20;

Your team is now created.&#x20;
