---
title: "Editing a Vault"
---


To edit a vault:

1. Navigate to the Settings > Workspace > Vaults page and click the **Actions** icon adjacent to the vault that you want to edit. From the options that appear, select **Edit**.\
   The Edit Vault page appears.
2. Update the **Vault URL**, **Tenant ID**, **Client ID**, and **Client Secret** as appropriate and click **Save**.
