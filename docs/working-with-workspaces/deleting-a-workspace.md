---
title: "Deleting a Workspace"
sidebar_position: 2
---


To delete a workspace:

1. Navigate to the Workspaces page by either clicking on your workspace in the Koodisi header and selecting **View all workspaces**, or by clicking **View workspaces** in the Koodisi Home page.
2. Click the ellipse (three dots) adjacent to a workspace, on the right.
3. Select **Delete**.

:::info
Once you delete a workspace, it's gone forever, and you can't undo it.
:::
