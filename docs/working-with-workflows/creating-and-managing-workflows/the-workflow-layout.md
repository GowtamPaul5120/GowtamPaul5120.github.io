The Workflow Layout provides a visual representation of the workflow's logic and sequence, making it easy to design and configure processes.

To view the workflow layout, navigate to the Applications panel and click on an application workflow.

## **Key Components**

The workflow layout comprises the following components:

* **Canvas Area**: A central space where activities are placed and connected in a workflow. &#x20;
* **Activity Panel**: A panel on the left containing all available triggers, activities, and connectors. Click the Activities icon in the main menu to access the Activities panel.
* **Error Panel**: The Error panel is located in the bottom-right corner of the page and displays errors identified during workflow validation or testing.
* **Workflow Toolbar**: The Workflow Toolbar appears above the canvas and offers a series of shortcuts to workflow-related actions.

## The Workflow Toolbar

* A tool to ensure the workflow configuration is correct before publishing or deployment.
