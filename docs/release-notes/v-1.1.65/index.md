### New and Improved Features

#### 1. Transfer Profile Ownership

Enables seamless transfer of profile ownership to another user, improving admin flexibility and continuity.

#### 2. Copy Mappings with Configuration

Allows copying mappings along with their configuration, speeding up reuse and reducing setup time.

#### 3. Scheduler and Messaging Deployment Comments

Deployment comments are now visible for scheduler and messaging, improving traceability and operational clarity.

#### 4. Notes Component Usability Enhancements

Improved interaction and readability for smoother note-taking and review.

#### 5. Group Usability Improvements

Updated group resizing behaviour for more intuitive and precise layout control.

***

### Bug Fixes

#### 1. Tab Switching Performance

Improved responsiveness when switching tabs, resulting in a smoother editing experience.

#### 2. Autosave in Multi-Tab Mode

Autosave now works reliably across multiple open tabs, preventing unintended data loss.
