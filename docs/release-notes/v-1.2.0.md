# V 1.2.0

## What's New in v1.2.0

_March 2026_

***

### Highlight: NX Agent (Beta)

Meet NX Agent — your AI-powered teammate, built right into the platform. No more switching tabs or hunting through docs. Just ask, design, or dig into your data — all in one place.

**Three ways to work with it:**

| Mode       | What it does                                                                                    |
| ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Ask**    | Chat with your integrations to get instant answers and insights                                 |
| **Design** | Plan out and manage your requirements conversationally                                          |
| **Triage** | Chat with your metrics, get an overall picture, and ask for specific attribute tags and details |

**Also shipping with NX Agent:**

* **Skill Agents** — Let agents manage skills and requirements on your behalf
* **Multi-agent changelog generation** — Automatically generate changelogs across multiple agents

> NX Agent is in beta. Reach out to your workspace admin to get access.

***

### Improvements

**For everyone**

* **Markdown in activity descriptions** — Format your activity descriptions with headers, lists, and more
* **Team visibility in workspace collaboration** — Browse and view teams directly from the workspace collaboration page

**For admins**

* **Organization-level permissions** — Set and manage access controls at the org level for tighter, more consistent governance across teams
* **Deployment approval notifications** — Get notified in your workspace the moment a deployment needs your approval — no more manual checking

***

### Developer Updates

* **Auto-generated publish tags** — Tags are now generated automatically from your current Git branch
* **Validation for missing Context** — Clear errors now surface early when Context is missing
* **Context support in Activity Testing** — Context now works in the Activity Testing flow; Mapper Activity testing is also enabled
