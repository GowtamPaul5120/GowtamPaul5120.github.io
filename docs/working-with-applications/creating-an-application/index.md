---
title: "Creating an Application"
sidebar_position: 2
---


To create an application:

1. Click **Create Application** in the Home page, or go to the **Applications** page from the main menu and click **Create Application**. The **Create Application** modal appears.
2. Click **New Application**. The **Create Application** modal appears.
3. Enter _HelloWorld_ in the **Name** field. Leave the other fields unchanged.
4. Click **Create**.
