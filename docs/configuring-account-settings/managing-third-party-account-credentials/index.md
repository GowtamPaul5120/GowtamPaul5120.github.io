Koodisi enables you to store third-party account credentials, which you can use to log into git-provider platforms and store your applications.

:::info
Koodisi securely stores and encrypts your credentials to ensure your privacy and personal safety.
:::

## Creating Credentials

To create and store a third-party credential on Koodisi:

1. Navigate to the Settings > Account > Credentials page and click **Create Credentials**.\
   The Add Credential modal appears.
2. Enter the **Credential Name** and select the name of the **Service Provider** whose credentials you want to save.
3. Enter the **Username** and **Password Token** in the fields provided.
4. Click **Create**.

## Editing Credentials

To edit a credential, navigate to the Settings > Account > Credentials page and click the **edit** button adjacent to the credentials you want to edit. Update the credentials as required and click **Update**.

## Deleting Credentials

To delete a credential, click the **Delete** button adjacent to the credentials you want to delete. Click **OK** in the warning message that appears.
