# Managing Your Profile

To view or update your profile, navigate to the Settings > Account > Profile page.

The Profile page comprises three tabs: General, Permissions, and Team. Click on each to review or update related details.

## Updating the General Details in Your Profile

In the General tab:

1. Update your **First Name** and **Last Name** details and click **Update Name**. (Optional)
2. Review the organizational **Role** and **Email** ID associated with your account.
3. Click the **Change Password** button to update your password.\
   \
   **Updating Your Password**
   1. Enter your **Current Password** and **New Password** in the fields provided.
   2. Retype your new password in the **Confirm New Password** field.
   3. Click **Change Password**.<br/>
4. Review the organization within which your account is created.

## Reviewing your Permissions

Click the **Permissions** tab to review your access privileges.&#x20;

The Permissions tab is divided into two sections: Organization and Workspace. Select each subtab to see the permissions you have at the corresponding level.

Click **Show inherent permissions** to see the access rights you gain automatically through your team memberships.
