---
title: Managing Your User Preferences
---


Use the Settings > Account > Preferences page to manage preferences associated with your account and customize your experience of Koodisi.

## Save Changes Preference

Use the Save Changes preference to specify how you want your changes saved in Koodisi: manually, automatically on mouse movement, or via a confirmation dialog. Select the appropriate option and click **Save Changes**.

## Mapper Tab Navigation Preference

Use the Mapper Tab Navigation Preference preference to specify whether you want the \<Tab> key to move your focus to the next expression in the UI or to use Intellisense to identify and populate the most likely value that must be added into the Mapper. Select the appropriate option and click **Save Changes**.
